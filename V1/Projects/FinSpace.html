FinSpace.Online

March 2020

Briefly, what is FinSpace.Online?

FinSpace.Online is a website I created with one other teammate during AthenaHack's 2020 Hackathon. It provides a platform for abused children to safely and comfortably find resources. It won 3 awards: Microsoft's Hack for Good, Facebook's Best Community Hack, and Google's Best Use of Google Cloud.

My Main Contribution

My main contribution to the site was the chat bot. I developed it using Microsoft Azure. It addresses Alcohol, Drug, Sexual, and Physical Abuse as well as some side effects such as depression. It takes into account typos and synonymns so it is a resource to children of many literacy levels. It has a conversation with the child rather than spitting out resources to build trust. It has many general conversation topics built in, as well as responses like "You can tell me anything" and "Everything said will stay between you and me" to assure them their parents, friends, or other adults in their life will never see their conversation. If any red flags are triggered, the child will immediately be ask "Would you like to speak to a real person?" and if they say yes, they will be put in contact with a child abuse helpline. If they reply with no, they are directed to testimonials of other people in similar situations to encourage them to speak up but not force them to. Speaking to a bot is a lot easier for a lot of children than speaking to a human, because the bot will not follow them for the rest of their life.
