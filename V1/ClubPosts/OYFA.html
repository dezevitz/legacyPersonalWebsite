Organization Of Young Filipino Americans

September 2018 - Present

What is the Organization of Young Filipino Americans?

The Organization of Young Filipino Americans (OYFA) promotes the culture and advancement of Filipinos-Americans on College Campuses. The organization itself hosts two culture events a year along with several smaller cultural/social events. OYFA's mission is to education the public on Filipino culture as well as creating a safe space for all members regardless of their race or religion.

My involment

I am very active in the club, as well as the cultural event, Barrio. In the Spring of 2019 and 2020, I participated in the event by performing in several traditional/modern dances as well as playing the National Anthem at the beginning of the event. I the Spring 2020 semester, I performed the lead female role in the Barrio 2020 skit, Clarity, which addressed many political issues in the 1960's Phillipines.
