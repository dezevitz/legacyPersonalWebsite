Growing in the Monroe Society

September 2018 - Present

What is the Monroe Society?

The Monroe Society is an organization that focuses on giving prospective students the real UVA experience through an overnight stay with a current UVA student.

My involment

My overnight stay with the monroe society was the reason why I decided to go to UVA. I had no desire to go to UVA at all but my host was very welcoming and this was the first time I was sure I wanted to got go to a University. I went from being a Prospective student to an officer to the Technology and PR chair to being the president!

My duties:

  • Creating and Maintaining relationships with UVA and the community
  • Maintain the UVA Monroe Society Website
  • Manage an executive team
  • Developed a more informative matching system
  • Improved transparency between exec and general members
